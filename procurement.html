PROCUREMENT DEPARTMENT

Procurement process includes the preparation and processing of a demand and the end the receipt

 Needs Assessment: Identify the specific IT equipment required based on business needs.
 Vendor Selection: Research and select reliable vendors or suppliers for the equipment.
 Budgeting: Determine the budget required for the procurement process.
 Request for Proposals (RFPs): Create RFPs to solicit bids from potential suppliers.
 Negotiation: Negotiate terms, prices, and contracts with selected vendors.
 Quality Assurance: Ensure the equipment meets quality standards and specifications.
