HUMAN RESOURCE DEPARTMENT



THE RESPONSIBLIES TO BE PERFORMED IN HUMAN RESOURCE


 Recruitment: human resource is responsible for attracting, interviewing,
and hiring qualified candidates for positions within the IT equipment
department. This includes posting job openings, conducting interviews,
and checking references.

 Onboarding: human resource ensures that new employees are properly on
boarded, which includes orientation, training, and completing necessary
paperwork.

 Performance Management: HR helps establish performance metrics,
conducts performance reviews, and implements performance
improvement plans when needed.

 Training and Development: HR is responsible for identifying training
needs and providing opportunities for skill development and career advancement
within the IT department
